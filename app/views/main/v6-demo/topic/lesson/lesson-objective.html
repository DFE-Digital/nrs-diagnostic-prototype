What will you do in this lesson


During this lesson you will:

  • learn how to write a formal email
  • identify the key features of a formal email
  • practice writing a formal email
  • be quizzed on what you have learnt throughout the lesson. You will receive a score at the end along with feedback

The following skills may be useful in your target job:

  • writing a job application
  • writing to a colleague at work (to request time off or arrange a meeting)
  • making a complaint or asking for a refund (usually to a company who has failed to provide their services as you would expect)
