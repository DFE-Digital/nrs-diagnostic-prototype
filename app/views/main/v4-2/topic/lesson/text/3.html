Article (Part 3 of 3)

In the final part of this lesson, you will learn how to end a formal email appropriately.

Once you have written the main body of your email, it is important to end the email appropriately with a closing statement and a formal sign off.

Closing

  • You must end your email with a brief summary statement and an appropriate closing such as ‘I look forward to hearing from you.’

Sign off

Finally, a formal email needs to sign-off appropriately.

If you do not know the name of person you are writing to, you must use:

  • Yours faithfully,
  • Your sincerely,

Or, if you know the name of the person you are writing to use:

  • Kind regards,
  • Best wishes,
