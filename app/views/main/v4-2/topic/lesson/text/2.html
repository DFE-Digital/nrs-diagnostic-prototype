Text (Part 2 of 3)

In the next section of this lesson, you will learn how to write the main part of an email.

The main part of your email needs to give clear information.

This lesson is demonstrating how to write an email for a job application, so you need to:

  • Explain why you are applying for the role.
  • Describe the experience and skills you currently have.

You should use formal language throughout your email. This means you should write using full sentences, paragraphs and correct spellings.
