Read this example and compare it to the added information you have written.

Remember, emails should be written using paragraphs, full sentences and correct spelling.

To: Sony Electronics
Subject: Jane Smith - Application for Sales Manager at Sony

Dear Sir/Madam

I am writing to apply for the position of Sales Manager at Sony Electronics. After reading the job description and requirements on your job portal, I believe they match with my own experiences and I think that I would make a valuable contribution to your organisation.

Currently, I am employed as a Retail Assistant at a large electronics store in London. Having been in this role for ten years, I have a wide range of experiences in sales and customer communications. I have recently completed an online course to develop my digital skills with the aim of improving my career prospects. I now want to apply these skills in an international company like Sony.
