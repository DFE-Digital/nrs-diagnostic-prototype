Writing an email

Lesson progress

The main body of your email needs to add information to the purpose for writing.

This is a job application, so you need to:

  • Explain why you are applying for the role.
  • Describe the experience and skills you currently have.
To: Sony Electronics
Subject: Jane Smith - Application for Sales Manager at Sony

Dear Sir/Madam

I am writing to apply for the position of Sales Manager at Sony Electronics.

After reading the job description and requirements on your job portal, I believe...

Currently, I am employed as…
Having been in this role for…
I have recently completed an...
I now want to apply these skills in....


Practice: Now pause and practise writing (or thinking about) the rest of this application email. You can use the sentence starters that have been provided to guide you.
