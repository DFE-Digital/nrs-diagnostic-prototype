How we are collecting data

In this case the research team is asking you to use a prototype and that usage will be recorded through a tool called Hotjar. Data collected includes where you click and scroll, what you do on screen (a reconstructed recording), answers you provide to popup questions and a questionnaire at the end. All data you input is anonymised by Hotjar and it reconstructs your session from that data and does not actually record your computer screen.

Hotjar WILL NOT be installing anything on your computer or phone.

Please accept all cookies so that Hotjar can collect data on what you do.

Please DO NOT put any personal identifiable data which could be used to identify you or anyone else. Examples include name, phone number, full address, email address, financial information, criminal convictions, school attended, teacher’s name etc. into the popup questions and questionnaire. In the open questions please do not make comments about others which may be libellous or defamatory.
