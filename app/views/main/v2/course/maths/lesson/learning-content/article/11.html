Arvio wants to make 60 kg of concrete. She has the following mixing ratio:

How much water will she need for 60kg of concrete?

Concrete recipe: makes 20kg
3kg cement
7kg sand
8kg stone
2l water

All the values have been multiplied by 3:

Concrete recipe: makes 60kg
9kg cement
21kg sand
24kg stone
6l water

So the answer to the question is:

She will need 6 litres of water
