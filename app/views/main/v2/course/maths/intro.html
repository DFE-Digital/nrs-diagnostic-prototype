Maths Functional Skills 2

Introduction

This course helps you develop your maths skills including proportion and handling data as well as providing you with the opportunity to apply them to real-life problems. The skills developed during this course will help learners get onto college courses, apprenticeships, further education and unlock more opportunities in the world of work.

What is the course structure?
The course is divided into different units which are delivered in a series of mini lessons. This quick and effective way of learning, provides plenty of opportunites for learners to practise key maths skills in a focused and practical way.
What will I learn on this course?
This course is broken down into the following chapters;
  • Integers
  • Decimals
  • Fractions
  • Percentages
  • Rounding
  • Money
  • Proportion
  • Algebra
  • Measures
  • Angles
  • 2D and 3D shapes
  • Area and Perimeter
  • Volume and Surface Area
  • Handling Data
  • Probability
How long is the course?
The course is designed in a way so that you can study in your spare time. On average, the couse is around 40 hours long, with each lesson lasting between 15 - 30 minutes to complete. Adult learning is taught in a relaxed and positive way, so don't be discouraged by the course length. Take your time and study when it suits you best.
How will this help me with getting a qualification?
Completing this course will fully prepare you to sit the two Functional Skills Level 2 exam papers (Calculator and Non-calculator exams), which you will need to pass in order to be awarded the qualification.
