English Functional Skills 2

Introduction

This course helps you develop your reading and writing skills including finding meaning in a range of texts and using different language features and devices.

What is the course structure?
The course is divided into different units and is delivered in a quick and effective way, providing opportunities to practise the key skills.
What will I learn on this course?
This course is broken down into the following chapters;
  • Finding Meaning in Text
  • Textual Features and Devices
  • Language and Structure
  • Using Correct Grammar
  • Punctuation
  • Spellings
  • Writing
How long is the course?
The course is designed in a way so that you can study in your spare time. On average, the couse is around 40 hours long, with each lesson lasting between 15 - 30 minutes to complete. Adult learning is taught in a relaxed and positive way, so don't be discouraged by the course length. Take your time and study when it suits you best.
How will this help me with getting a qualification?
Completing this course will fully prepare you to sit the Reading and Writing Functional Skills Level 2 exam papers, which you will need to pass in order to be awarded the qualification.
