It is also really important that you know how to write an email in an appropriate style for your target audience. So, before you begin writing your email, you must ask yourself the following:

  • What is the purpose of your email?
    Are you writing a formal work-related email or an informal email to a friend?
  • What are the key features you must include?
  • What kind of language should you use?
