Closing a formal email

15 of 16

A formal email needs to use a sign-off such as:

  • Yours faithfully
  • Yours sincerely
  • Kind regards

You can also add a brief summary or ‘next steps’ paragraph to conclude your email.

To: Sony Electronics
Subject: Jane Smith - Application for Sales Manager at Sony

Dear Sir/Madam

I have attached a cover letter and CV for your consideration. Please take a moment to read through them to get a better picture of who I am. I look forward to hearing from you and I hope that I get a chance to discuss the role in more depth with a member of the Sony team.

Yours sincerely,
Jane Smith
